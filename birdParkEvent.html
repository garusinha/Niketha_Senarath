Easter Eggs-travaganza

Join us as we hatch our very first Easter Eggs-travaganza at Bird Paradise! Participate in our egg-citing Easter egg hunts and redeem eggs-tra special prizes.

Camping with the Penguins

Embark on an extraordinary journey as you revel in exclusive avian encounters and guided tours during the day. As the sun sets, unwind in style within your very own air-conditioned tent, with a view of the mesmerizing Southern Lights at Ocean Network Express Penguin Cove!

Wild About You

There’s much to be discovered in our wildlife parks — from majestic animals to familiar flavours, and new experiences designed specially for you, we are wild about creating unique memories for and with you. So, take a sneak peek to unravel what awaits on your next adventure!
