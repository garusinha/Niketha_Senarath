Why You should vist Singapore ZooPark?

Wildlife Experience: Singapore Zoo offers a chance to see a wide variety of animals from around the world in environments that closely mimic their natural habitats.

Interactive Experiences: Singapore Zoo offers numerous interactive experiences such as feeding sessions, animal shows, and behind-the-scenes tours, providing visitors with opportunities to learn more about the animals and their behaviors.

Beautiful Setting: The zoo is set amidst lush tropical greenery, creating a serene and picturesque environment for visitors to enjoy.

Free car parking

Cost: $17 (free for members)

Parking is available at the Zoo Garage at 35th & Girard Ave, Tiger Parking Lot

Supper Enviorment

The zoo provides complimentary shuttle services from certain MRT stations to the zoo entrance.
