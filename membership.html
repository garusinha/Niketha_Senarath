Welcome to our Membership Program


Become a part of our community and unlock a world of adventure, education, and conservation. By joining our membership program , you not only gain unlimited access to Mandai Zoo Park and Jurong Bird Park, but you also play a vital role in supporting our efforts in wildlife conservation, research, and education.

Unlimited Access: Enjoy unlimited visits to Mandai Zoo Park and Jurong Bird Park for a full year. Come as often as you like and explore the wonders of the animal kingdom.

Exclusive Events: Get priority access to special events, behind-the-scenes tours, and members-only experiences. From animal encounters to guided tours, there's always something exciting happening for our members.

Savings and Discounts: Receive discounts on park admission, food, merchandise, and select experiences. Make the most of your visits with exclusive member-only deals and offers.

Support Conservation: Receive discounts on park admission, food, merchandise, and select experiences. Make the most of your visits with exclusive member-only deals and offers.

Membership Options

Choose the membership plan that best suits your needs

Individual Membership

Perfect for individuals who love wildlife and want to enjoy the parks on their own.

Family Membership

Ideal for families who enjoy spending quality time together and exploring nature.

Student/Senior Membership

Special discounted rates available for students and seniors. Must present valid identification.

Corporate Membership

Enhance your company's corporate social responsibility efforts by providing your employees with access to nature and wildlife experiences.

How to Join


Becoming a member is easy! Simply visit our website or stop by the membership counter at Mandai Zoo Park or Jurong Bird Park to sign up today. Your membership will be activated instantly, allowing you to start enjoying all the benefits right away.
